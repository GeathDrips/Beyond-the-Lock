Beyond the Lock

A spacial investigation on public access

Beyond the Lock is a digital publication, challenging traditional methods of the book form and publishing. Through 3D scanning techniques and Gaussian Splatting methods, these visual investigations explore sites found throughout Montreal that exhibit varying degrees of public access.

Produced in an edition of 25, this publication presents a digital archive of 3D scans with an expiry date of November 2026. This temporality urges readers to more carefully consider the ephemerality of access in their own daily lives.

Coordinates are given for each site, encouraging readers to visit the sites themselves, to observe the level of access or lack thereof, and bear witness to how the space has changed over time.
